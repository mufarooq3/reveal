WHAT WE DO AT REVEAL?

ReVeal(Recognation, Vision and Learning) Research group ReVeal is a research group that focuses on applied research in Visual Recognation, Computer vision and Machine learning for solving real world large scale complex problem

BootCamp

Bootcamp consists of lecture sessions which will introduce basic theoratical aspects from algorithmic perspective and hands-on lab sessions to implement in Python Data Science Stack. Participants are trained to work on real-life datasets, and mentored to participate in three different Kaggle competions
